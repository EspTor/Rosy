# Economy & Resource System

## Economic Actors

Agents can be: Producer, Consumer, Worker, Business Owner, Investor, Landlord

## Resources

- Physical goods (tangible, storable)
- Services (intangible, consumed immediately)
- Currency (fungible tokens)
- Raw materials (inputs for production)

## Production

Businesses convert labor + capital + materials → goods via production function.
Production chains: Farm → Mill → Bakery → Retail → Consumer

## Markets & Pricing

Prices emerge from supply/demand equilibrium.
Dynamic adjustment based on inventory, demand curves.
Agents shop based on need, budget, price comparison, convenience.

## Currency & Finance

Fixed money supply initially. Banking: deposits, loans, credit cards.
Taxation: income, sales, property taxes fund public goods.

## Labor Market

Jobs with requirements, salary, schedule.
Agents apply, get hired, can be fired.
Unemployment affects happiness and stress.

## Economic Debugging

Track: price history, employment rates, business success rates, wealth distribution (Gini coefficient).
