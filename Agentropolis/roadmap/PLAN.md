# Development Roadmap

## Phase 0: Foundations (Months 1-2)
- Week 1-2: Project setup, CI/CD, DB schema
- Week 3-4: Basic simulation engine, spatial grid, ECS
- Week 5-6: Persistence layer, snapshots, WAL
- Week 7-8: Agent brain interface, rule-based brain

## Phase 1: Single Agent Town (Months 3-4)
- Week 9-10: Town structure (7 districts, 50 locations)
- Week 11-12: Basic agents & jobs, needs system
- Week 13-14: Simple economy (supermarket, transactions)
- Week 15-16: Observer dashboard (Streamlit)

## Phase 2: Social Dynamics (Months 5-6)
- Week 17-18: Relationship system
- Week 19-20: Conversation system
- Week 21-22: Family & long-term bonds
- Week 23-24: Group dynamics (businesses, clubs)

## Phase 3: Economy (Months 7-8)
- Week 25-26: Production system, supply chains
- Week 27-28: Advanced market (dynamic pricing)
- Week 29-30: Labor market (hiring, performance)
- Week 31-32: Finance & property (banking, ownership)

## Phase 4: Observer & Scale (Months 9-10)
- Week 33-34: Agent connector API v1
- Week 35-36: LLM brain backup
- Week 37-38: Scaling (parallelization, partitioning)
- Week 39-40: Comprehensive observability

## Phase 5: Polish & Open (Months 11-12)
- Week 41-42: Security hardening
- Week 43-44: Documentation & SDK
- Week 45-46: Performance optimization
- Week 47-48: Public beta launch
