# Agent Interactions & Social Dynamics

## Interaction Types

1. **Direct Communication**: Conversations with turn-based dialog
2. **Economic Transactions**: Buy/sell/gift with price negotiation
3. **Collaborative Actions**: Multi-agent tasks (moving furniture, starting business)
4. **Conflict & Competition**: Resource competition, rivalry, aggression
5. **Social Relationship Formation**: Stages from stranger to friend to romantic
6. **Information Spread**: Gossip, rumors, beliefs
7. **Group Formation**: Families, businesses, clubs, gangs
8. **Role & Status Systems**: Social roles, hierarchy, prestige

## Relationship Development

Stranger → Acquaintance → Friend → Close Friend/Romantic → Enemy/Rival

Each stage has thresholds for trust, affection, familiarity.

## Social Network

Agents form a complete graph with weighted edges (relationship strength).
Metrics: centrality, betweenness, clustering coefficient.

## Emergent Phenomena

- Economic: specialization, supply chains, inequality
- Social: factions, social mobility, cultural transmission
- Political: coalitions, corruption, social movements
- Urban: gentrification, traffic patterns, public goods

## Debugging Tools

- Conversation logger
- Relationship matrix (heat map)
- Social network graph visualization
- Event timeline filtering
