# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Positive: empathy, kindness, respect, constructive feedback
Unacceptable: harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, publishing private info

## Enforcement

Report to conduct@agentropolis.org. We will acknowledge within 48h.
