# Summer Vacation 2026 Road Trip

**Destination:** Drammen, Norway → Agliano Terme, Italy (and back)  
**Vehicle:** VW ID.4 Pro (electric, ~520 km WLTP range)  
**Driving limit:** 3‑5 hours per day (~200‑300 km depending on terrain & speed)  
**Goal:** Enjoy scenic drives, frequent charging stops, and explore en‑route attractions.

---

## 1. Vehicle & Charging Notes
- **WLTP range:** ~520 km (real‑world ~400‑450 km mixed).  
- **Charging speed:** up to 125 kW DC (≈10‑80 % in ~30 min).  
- **Planning:** Aim for 150‑200 km legs between reliable DC fast chargers (IONITY, Fastned, Shell Recharge, Tesla Supercharger (via adapter), etc.).  
- **Apps:** Use *A Better Routeplanner* (ABRP) or *PlugShare* with filters for CCS2 connectors.

## 4. Daily Planning Tips
- **Start early** (after breakfast) to maximize daylight for scenic stops.  
- **Charge to 80‑90 %** at each stop to preserve battery health and reduce time.  
- **Leverage destination charging** (hotels, agriturismi) when available – plug in overnight.  
- **Check weather** – mountain passes may have snow early/late season; adjust routes if needed.  
- **Keep a buffer** of ~50 km range for detours or unexpected closures.  
- **Document** each day in your Obsidian vault (daily notes) with photos, mileage, and charging cost.
