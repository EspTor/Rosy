## 1. Executive Summary

NordicEPOD AS is a Norwegian industrial company that manufactures **pre‑commissioned, containerized Electrical Power Optimization Devices (EPODs)** for critical infrastructure, primarily data centers. Its factory in Hanekleiva, opened on 2 September 2024, is the largest EPOD production facility in Europe and the first in Europe to use mobile assembly lines. The company emphasizes standardization, digital traceability, and fast delivery to meet the high‑availability demands of the data‑center industry.

## 3. What Is an EPOD?

An **EPOD** is a self‑contained, factory‑built unit that integrates:

- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPSs)**
- **Switchgear and power distribution**
- **Cooling systems**
- **Control systems**

EPODs are engineered to sit between the utility power grid and a data center’s critical IT equipment, providing:

- Backup power during outages
- Power quality optimization (voltage regulation, harmonics mitigation)
- Simplified deployment (pre‑commissioned and tested in the factory)

Each EPOD is built to a **standardized design**, enabling faster installation and predictable performance.

## 4. Key Differentiators

1. **Digital Passport**  
   - Every EPOD has a complete digital record accessible via the client portal.  
   - Stores all component data, test results, certificates, and lifecycle information.  
   - Enables full traceability from manufacturing to end‑of‑life.

2. **Live Broadcasting & On‑Demand Recordings**  
   - Customers can watch remote testing live from anywhere.  
   - Recordings are archived in the digital passport for later review.

3. **Pre‑Commissioning**  
   - EPODs are fully tested and configured in the factory before shipment.  
   - Reduces on‑site installation time and risk.

4. **Mobile Assembly Lines**  
   - First factory in Europe to use mobile assembly lines for EPOD production.  
   - Increases throughput and flexibility.

5. **Sustainability Focus**  
   - Green production processes and durable, long‑life equipment.

6. **Large‑Scale Facility**  
   - 12 000 m² (129 000 sq ft) factory – the largest of its kind in Europe.
